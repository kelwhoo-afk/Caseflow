# Summit Commercial Auto — Auto Accident Intake Checklist

## First-pass intake goals

Collect enough facts to determine:
1. Whether there is an injury.
2. Whether the accident is inside coverage.
3. Which auto subtype applies.
4. Whether the case fits Summit Commercial Auto or should be referred to another AutoCase Network firm.
5. Whether the caller needs attorney review, referral, resource response, or more information.

## Minimum required fields

- Caller full name
- Phone number
- Email, if available
- Preferred language
- Relationship to injured person
- Accident date
- Accident location: city, county, state, freeway/intersection if available
- Accident subtype
- Injury description
- Medical treatment: none, planned, urgent care, ER, hospital, surgery
- Minor involved: yes/no/unknown
- Insurance contact: yes/no/unknown
- Police report: yes/no/unknown
- Photos/video/witnesses: yes/no/unknown

## Auto subtype prompts

### Rear-end / clear-liability collision
Ask:
- Were you stopped or moving?
- Were you hit from behind?
- Did police make a report?
- Are you experiencing neck, back, or head pain?
- Did insurance contact you?

### Rideshare accident
Ask:
- Was Uber or Lyft involved?
- Were you a passenger, driver, pedestrian, or another driver?
- Was the app on or was a ride in progress?
- Which insurance companies have contacted you?

### Commercial vehicle accident
Ask:
- Was the other vehicle a company car, delivery van, work truck, or fleet vehicle?
- Do you know the company name?
- Did you go to the ER or hospital?
- Has a company insurer contacted you?

### Multi-car / disputed liability
Ask:
- How many vehicles were involved?
- Is anyone blaming you?
- Was there a police report?
- Are multiple insurance companies involved?

### Hit-and-run / uninsured
Ask:
- Did the other driver leave the scene?
- Do you know if they had insurance?
- Did you file a police report?
- Do you know whether you have uninsured motorist coverage?

### Property damage only
Ask:
- Was anyone injured?
- Are you seeking only vehicle repair or property damage reimbursement?

## Summit Commercial Auto fit note

Best-fit cases for this firm:
- delivery van, company vehicle, work truck, fleet vehicle accidents
- ER, hospitalization, surgery, fracture, brain/spine injury, or death
- commercial insurer or employer representative contacted caller
- high-severity auto cases even if not fully investigated yet

## Referral note

If the case is a better fit for another network firm, do not share client information until consent is granted.
