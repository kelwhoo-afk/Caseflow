# Summit Commercial Auto — Case Acceptance and Referral Criteria

## Accept criteria

Summit Commercial Auto should generally accept or request attorney review when:

- delivery van, company vehicle, work truck, fleet vehicle accidents
- ER, hospitalization, surgery, fracture, brain/spine injury, or death
- commercial insurer or employer representative contacted caller
- high-severity auto cases even if not fully investigated yet

Additional required signals:
- Injury is mentioned.
- The caller is in coverage or the case has statewide coverage fit.
- The facts do not require a better-matched specialist firm.
- Attorney capacity is available or urgent review is justified.

## Priority triggers

Escalate priority when any of the following are present:
- Minor or child involved.
- ER, hospital, surgery, fracture, brain/spine injury, or severe ongoing symptoms.
- Commercial vehicle or employer-owned vehicle.
- Insurance company is pressuring the caller.
- Multiple parties are blaming each other.
- Hit-and-run or uninsured/underinsured driver.
- Caller asks "Do I have a case?" or requests legal evaluation.

## Decline/resource triggers

Use a decline/resource path when:
- No one was injured.
- Property damage only.
- Caller only wants repair reimbursement.
- Accident occurred outside service area and no network firm applies.
- Caller refuses consent for referral and no other workflow is available.

## Refer-out criteria

- simple Bay Area rear-end with moderate injury -> BayBridge Auto Injury
- multi-car dispute without commercial or severe injury -> Pacific Complex Collision
- hit-and-run or uninsured issue without commercial defendant -> Pacific Complex Collision
- Southern California standard/moderate auto case -> Vista Auto Justice
- no injury / property damage only -> Decline/resource path

## Attorney review threshold

Send to attorney review when:
- Case is a possible fit but missing facts prevent acceptance.
- Minor involved and injury is possible.
- Caller asks for case-strength evaluation.
- High-priority facts are present but the matching firm should confirm.
- Capacity is limited and attorney must approve intake.

## Retrieval examples

- Query: "A FedEx delivery van hit me in San Jose and I went to the ER." -> retrieve this firm's acceptance criteria.
- Query: "I was rear-ended in Oakland and my neck hurts." -> retrieve referral criteria and partner match.
