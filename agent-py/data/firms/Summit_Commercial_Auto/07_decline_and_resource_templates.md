# Summit Commercial Auto — Resource and Decline Response Templates

## Property damage only

"Based on what you shared, this appears to involve vehicle or property damage without an injury. This may not require a personal-injury attorney. We can provide general resources for handling a vehicle damage claim, but we will not use attorney review capacity for this intake."

## Outside coverage but network fit

"This may be outside our direct coverage, but it may be a fit for a trusted partner firm in our network. Your information is confidential and will not be shared unless you give permission."

## Missing injury information

"To route this correctly, I need to know whether anyone was injured or received medical treatment."

## Missing contact information

"What is the best phone number and email for the attorney team or referral partner to follow up if you choose to proceed?"
