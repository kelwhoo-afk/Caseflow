# Vista Auto Justice — Intake Call Script

## Opening

"I'm sorry that happened. I can collect the key details for attorney review. I am not a lawyer and cannot determine whether you have a case, but I can help get the right information to the right team."

## Core questions

1. "Where and when did the accident happen?"
2. "Was anyone injured or treated by urgent care, the ER, or a doctor?"
3. "Was a child or minor involved?"
4. "Was a commercial vehicle, Uber/Lyft, multiple cars, hit-and-run, or uninsured driver involved?"
5. "Has any insurance company contacted you?"
6. "What is the best phone number for the firm to follow up?"

## Follow-up questions based on retrieved context

### If commercial vehicle
"Do you know the company name on the vehicle?"

### If ER/hospital
"Are you still receiving medical treatment?"

### If minor involved
"Was the child injured or evaluated by a medical provider?"

### If insurance pressure
"Have they asked for a recorded statement or for you to sign anything?"

### If referral likely
"Based on what you shared, this may be a better fit for a trusted partner firm. Your information is confidential, and we will not share it without your permission."

## Closing for attorney review

"Thank you. I will prepare this for attorney review and mark the missing details so the team can follow up."

## Closing for referral consent

"Do you consent to us sharing your case summary and contact information with the recommended partner firm so they can review whether they may be able to help?"
